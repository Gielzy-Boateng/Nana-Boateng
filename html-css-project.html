WEST COMFORT INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

West Comfort International School is a "prepared environment" for learning and academic excellence. Our facilities,staff and curriculum provides students with all the resources needed to excel.

West Comfort International helps expose children to a rich and varied teaching and learning environment which seeks to broaden their outlook; encourage the best examples of Christian morality and service to others and to develop skills which will enable them to compete favourably and be global agents of change.
